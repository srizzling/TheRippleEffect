Resources

We are currently collecting... ...documents, pictures and videos that can be used as an educational resource. Teachers are encouraged to use this material to help students understand the strong links between present day water uses, water pollution, and our lives. Below are examples of the kinds of archives a care group would be encouraged to make available as a public resource. We are currently building a searchable catalog to assist with this...

Pahaoa River

The River Dog Film Trailer below shows one man's fight to save his beloved Pahaoa River. The Pahaoa is just one of many rural waterways that are filled with cattle and sheep that are destroying what remains of its health and natural beauty.
